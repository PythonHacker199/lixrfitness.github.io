Rules

Simple progression

  • Hit all reps cleanly before increasing difficulty.
  • Add tempo, pauses, or extra sets before chasing harder skills.
  • Keep one or two reps in reserve on most strength work.

Recovery

What matters most

  • Sleep enough or the program stops working.
  • Walk, stretch, and hydrate on low-energy days.
  • Protect wrists, elbows, and shoulders with clean form.
